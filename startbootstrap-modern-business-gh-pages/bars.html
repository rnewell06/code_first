Bars and Pubs

Dirty Onion

Whether it’s food, drink or entertainment we have it all at The Dirty Onion. Home to two bars, one restaurant and one of the biggest beer gardens in Belfast, we are sure to keep you entertained all year round. We offer entertainment 7 nights a week, with a strong focus on authentic traditional Irish music. Oh, and we are free entry every night! A strong partnership with local Irish music school An Droichead has helped us bring together a fantastic arrangement of Irish music.Contemporary live bands and acoustic acts also offer fantastic additional entertainment at the weekends.

Crown

The Crown Liquor Saloon, also known as the Crown Bar, is a pub in Great Victoria Street in Belfast, Northern Ireland. Refurbished in 1885, and at least twice since, it is an outstanding example of a Victorian gin palace, and one of Northern Ireland's best-known pubs. It is owned by the National Trust and is leased to Mitchells & Butlers who run it as a Nicholson's pub.

Harp Bar

The Harp Bar, incorporating the Dunville & Swinging Diddy Lounges is decorated in plush red velvet fabrics and adorned with antique furnishings resonant with Victorian Belfast. Walls and cabinets feature rare memorabilia inspired by the building’s origins as a bonded warehouse – the headquarters of The Old Bushmills Distillery Company.
